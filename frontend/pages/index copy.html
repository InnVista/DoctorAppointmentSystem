About Us

Welcome to the AI-Powered Health Monitoring Dashboard, a smart and intuitive solution designed to transform healthcare through technology. Our platform empowers patients and healthcare professionals with real-time insights, smart symptom checking, and AI-driven analysis of physiological data. By integrating patient profiles, medical reports, and predictive models, we aim to improve early diagnosis, monitor chronic conditions, and enhance decision-making in clinical care. Developed as a final-year project by a passionate student team, this system combines modern web technologies with artificial intelligence to deliver a secure, user-friendly, and scalable health management solution. Whether you're a patient tracking your wellness or a doctor managing appointments and reports, our dashboard brings everything together in one efficient platform.
